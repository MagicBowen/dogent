# Research Report

## Introduction

Template for structured research reports that present background, methods, findings, and conclusions with citations.

## Writing Requirements

- Include clear research questions or objectives.
- Describe data sources and methods transparently.
- Present findings with evidence and citations.
- Distinguish between results, analysis, and recommendations.
- Provide a references section with links at the end.

## Best Practices

- Start with an executive summary for non-technical readers.
- Use headings and subheadings for scannability.
- Cross-check claims against multiple sources.
- Keep terminology consistent; define terms when first introduced.

## Output Template

```markdown
## Background

## Research Questions / Objectives

## Methodology

## Findings

## Analysis / Discussion

## Conclusion

## Recommendations (Optional)

## References
```
