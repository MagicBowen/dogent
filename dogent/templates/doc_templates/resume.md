# Resume

## Introduction

Professional resume template for job applications. Use for concise, scannable documents that highlight experience, skills, and achievements.

## Writing Requirements

- Keep the document to one or two pages unless the user specifies otherwise.
- Use bullet points for experience and achievements; start bullets with action verbs.
- Quantify impact where possible (metrics, scope, results).
- Avoid long paragraphs; keep sentences short and direct.

## Best Practices

- Tailor the summary and skills to the target role.
- Order experience by most recent first.
- Remove irrelevant or outdated details.
- Use consistent formatting for dates, titles, and locations.

## Output Template

```markdown
## Header
- Name
- Title
- Contact (email, phone, location, links)

## Summary
- 2-4 lines highlighting role, expertise, and impact

## Skills
- Core skills grouped by category

## Experience
- Role, Company, Location, Dates
  - Achievement bullet 1
  - Achievement bullet 2

## Projects (Optional)
- Project name + brief impact

## Education
- Degree, School, Dates

## Certifications (Optional)
```
